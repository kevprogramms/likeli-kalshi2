## Understanding Fees

### Deposit Fee (0-3%)

- Charged **immediately** when you deposit
- Goes directly to the manager
- Reduces your effective deposit amount

**Example**: 
- You deposit 1,000 USDC
- Fund has 2% deposit fee
- Manager receives 20 USDC
- 980 USDC enters the vault
- You receive shares worth 980 USDC

### Performance Fee (0-30%)

- Charged **only on profits**
- Calculated when fund closes
- Paid automatically on first redemption

**Example**:
- Initial AUM: 10,000 USDC
- Final balance: 12,000 USDC
- Profit: 2,000 USDC
- Performance fee (20%): 400 USDC
- Distributable: 11,600 USDC

If there's no profit, there's no performance fee.

## How to Deposit

### Step 1: Find a Fund
Browse funds at `/vaults` and evaluate:
- Manager track record
- Strategy description
- Fee structure
- Timeline (trading period)
- Current AUM

### Step 2: Connect Wallet
Click **Connect Wallet** and select your Solana wallet.

### Step 3: Deposit USDC

1. Navigate to the fund's page
2. Click **Deposit**
3. Enter USDC amount
4. Review:
   - Deposit fee deduction
   - Shares you'll receive
5. Click **Confirm Deposit**
6. Sign the transaction

### Step 4: Receive Shares
After confirmation:
- USDC is transferred to the vault
- Deposit fee is sent to manager
- Share tokens are minted to your wallet

## How to Withdraw

### During Open Stage
If the fund is still in Open stage:

1. Navigate to the fund page
2. Click **Withdraw**
3. Enter share amount
4. You receive proportional USDC
5. Sign and confirm

### During Closed Stage (Redemption)
After the fund is finalized:

1. Navigate to the fund page
2. Click **Redeem Shares**
3. Enter share amount (or max)
4. Review USDC you'll receive
5. Sign and confirm

The first redeemer triggers the performance fee payment. Subsequent redemptions proceed normally.

## FAQ

**Q: Can the manager steal my funds?**
A: No. The program enforces that managers can only trade on DFlow markets. They cannot transfer USDC to themselves.

**Q: What if the manager loses everything?**
A: You would receive little or no USDC at redemption. This is trading risk.

**Q: Can I withdraw early?**
A: Only during Open stage. During Trading, funds are locked.

**Q: What happens if markets don't resolve?**
A: The manager must wait or exit at market price before finalizing.
